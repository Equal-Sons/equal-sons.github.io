Brand Voice
How we sound matters as much as what we say. These guidelines ensure consistency across all communications.
Voice Attributes
Confident, not arrogant
We know what we're good at and say so clearly. But we lead with value, not ego. We let the work speak.
Warm, not casual
We're approachable and human, but professional. We can use contractions and speak directly without being sloppy or overly familiar.
Active, not passive
We use action verbs. We say what we do, not what we might help with. "We build" not "We can help you build."
Clear, not clever
Clarity beats creativity every time. If someone has to work to understand what we mean, we've failed. Simple language, direct sentences.
Words We Use
Build, create, make, craft
Partner, collaborate, alongside
Momentum, forward, move
Clarity, direction, focus
Real, tangible, actual
Words We Avoid
Leverage, synergy, optimize (corporate jargon)
Revolutionary, disruptive, game-changing (hyperbole)
Solutions, offerings, deliverables (vague)
World-class, best-in-class, cutting-edge (empty superlatives)
